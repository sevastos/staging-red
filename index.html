What is NG911?

Put simply, NG911 is a next generation Internet Protocol (IP)-based system that allows digital information (e.g., voice, photos, videos, text messages) to flow seamlessly from the public, through the 911 network, and on to emergency responders. This updated network is currently being deployed at city, county or state levels all across the country.





REDBOX and NG911

REDBOX is built with the specifications of NG911 in mind, so that it is able to fully utilize this next generation emergency network anywhere it is available and anywhere it becomes available - as soon as that happens. REDBOX reaches its complete potential when under NG911 coverage, wherever that is available. However, it also benefits from different fall-back mechanisms that enable its functionalities even in less advanced networks.
